## Quick Reference Guide

### Training Zones
- **Maximum Strength**: 85-100% 1RM (Neural drive)
- **Strength-Speed**: 60-80% 1RM (Power development)
- **Speed-Strength**: 30-60% 1RM (Optimal power zone)
- **Speed**: 0-30% 1RM (Maximum velocity)

### Recovery Protocols
- **Daily**: HRV monitoring, sleep tracking, nutrition timing
- **Weekly**: Massage, contrast showers, yoga sessions
- **Phase**: Deload weeks (4, 8), assessment periods

### Competition Integration
- **Weeks 1-3**: Technical development, base building
- **Weeks 4-6**: Power development, initial competitions
- **Weeks 7-9**: Elite integration, high-level matches
- **Weeks 10-12**: Championship peaking, major competitions
