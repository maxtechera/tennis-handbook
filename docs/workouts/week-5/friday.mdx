## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Tennis Dynamic Agility Drills
- Rapid directional changes
- Explosive transitions between movements
- Focus on acceleration and deceleration
- Simulate match movement demands
- High intensity court movement patterns

### Trap-bar Deadlift (4×5)
- Heavy weight progression from deload
- Reduced reps for strength focus
- Explosive drive through hips
- Perfect form with challenging load
- Progressive overload from Week 4
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Deadlift Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=op9kVnSso6Q)

### Hip Thrusts (4×8)
- Explosive upward thrust movement
- Maximum glute activation
- Control eccentric phase
- Full range of motion
- Power development focus
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Hip Thrust Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xDmFkJxPzeM)

### Landmine Press (3×8 per side)
- Stable stance throughout movement
- Controlled tempo both directions
- Unilateral strength development
- Core stability under load
- Progressive resistance from previous weeks
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Landmine Press](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kR8gZcCK5-8)

### Rotational Med-Ball Slams (3×10)
- Explosive full-body movement
- Maximum rotational power
- Quick reset between reps
- Core and hip engagement
- Tennis-specific power development
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Power Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K8aGBKy3a1k)

### Farmer's Walk (2×40m)
- Stable posture throughout
- Firm grip challenge
- Controlled walking pace
- Core engagement to prevent lean
- Functional strength development
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Farmer's Walk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p8FWv6bUJ7w)

### Reactive Plyometrics (3×6)
- Quick landings with explosive rebound
- Focus on reactive strength
- Minimal ground contact time
- Perfect landing mechanics
- Progressive volume from previous weeks
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

## Key Focus Points

- **Dynamic Agility**: Rapid directional changes with explosive transitions
- **Heavy Deadlifts**: Trap-bar deadlift 4×5 with heavy weight
- **Explosive Hip Drive**: Hip thrusts with explosive upward movement
- **Unilateral Press**: Landmine press with stable stance
- **Rotational Power**: Med-ball slams with full-body movement
- **Reactive Training**: Plyometrics with quick landings

## Notes

Friday emphasizes explosive full-body power development with dynamic tennis agility training.
