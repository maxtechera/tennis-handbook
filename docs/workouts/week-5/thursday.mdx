## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Aerobic Bike Intervals (5×3 min)
- Moderate intensity with controlled breathing
- Maintain steady effort throughout intervals
- 90 seconds active recovery between sets
- Focus on rhythmic pedaling pattern
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Hanging Leg Raises (3×12)
- Secure grip on pull-up bar
- Controlled knee-to-chest motion
- 2-second hold at top position
- Slow, controlled descent
- Focus on lower abdominal engagement
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Abs Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Side Plank (3×45 sec)
- Perfect alignment from head to feet
- Stack hips and shoulders vertically
- Maintain neutral spine throughout
- Breathe normally during hold
- Focus on lateral core stability
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Core Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Sun Salutations
- Flow through dynamic sequence
- Emphasize smooth transitions
- Target hip flexors and shoulders
- Hold positions briefly at end ranges
- Focus on controlled movement

### Tennis Technical Drills
- Skill precision with tactical control
- Emphasize placement over power
- Work on stroke consistency
- Practice court positioning
- Focus on technical refinement

### Zone-2 Aerobic Bike (30 min)
- Comfortable, sustainable pace
- Maintain conversational breathing
- 60-70% maximum heart rate
- Focus on pedaling efficiency
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

## Key Focus Points

- **Aerobic Intervals**: Moderate intensity with controlled breathing
- **Core Development**: Extended plank holds and hanging leg raises
- **Mobility Flow**: Sun salutations and full-body stretches
- **Technical Precision**: Skill refinement with tactical control
- **Aerobic Base**: Zone-2 aerobic maintenance
- **Tendon Care**: Deep stretching and mobility work

## Notes

Thursday serves as active recovery with aerobic development and technical tennis skill refinement.
