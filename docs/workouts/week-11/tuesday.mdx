## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Medicine Ball Explosive Push
- Lie on bench with ball on chest
- Explosively throw ball straight up
- Partner catches or self-catch
- Reset with control
- Maximum explosive intent

### Speed Rotation Drill
- Stand in athletic position
- Hold light weight or band
- Rotate rapidly side to side
- Keep hips stable
- Focus on trunk speed

### Plyo Push-ups (Competition Version)
- Standard push-up position
- Lower controlled
- Explode up maximally
- Land soft and reset
- Perfect form priority

### Band Pulls (Fast)
- Light resistance band
- Pull band apart rapidly
- Focus on scapular speed
- Maintain posture
- High frequency movement

### Core Activation
- Plank or dead bug position
- Focus on breathing
- Maintain perfect stability
- Competition-ready tension
- Mental focus practice
