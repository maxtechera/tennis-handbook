## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Bench Press (Maintenance)
- Use 70% of 1RM (feels moderate)
- Focus on perfect technique
- Smooth controlled movement
- Quality over intensity
- Maintain strength patterns

### Medicine Ball Chest Pass (Quality)
- Focus on technique perfection
- Smooth powerful release
- Consistent ball placement
- Quality movement patterns
- Prepare for competition

### Light Plyometric Push-ups
- Gentle explosive movement
- Focus on landing control
- Perfect form priority
- No maximum effort
- Maintain power patterns

### Band Pull-aparts
- Light resistance band
- Pull band apart smoothly
- Squeeze shoulder blades
- Activation focus
- Prepare posterior chain

### Perfect Form Push-ups
- Textbook push-up form
- Control both directions
- Full range of motion
- Quality over quantity
- Technical refinement
