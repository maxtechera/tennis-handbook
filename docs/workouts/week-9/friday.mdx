## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Depth Jump to Sprint
- Step off box, land, immediately sprint 3m
- Combines reactive strength with acceleration
- Minimal ground contact before sprint
- Express deadlift power in dynamic movement
- Full effort on both components
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

### Lateral Bounds (Complex)
- Explosive lateral jumps
- Land and stick on opposite leg
- Express hip thrust power laterally
- Focus on distance and control
- Tennis-specific lateral power
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

### Burpee to Jump
- Full burpee down and up
- Explosive jump at top
- Express push press power through full body
- Continuous movement pattern
- Cardiovascular and power challenge
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Conditioning](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Mixed Plyometrics Circuit
- Combine different plyometric movements
- Vertical jumps, lateral bounds, depth jumps
- 50 seconds continuous work
- Change movements every 10-15 seconds
- Maintain power throughout
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

### Tennis Movement Patterns
- Court-specific movement combinations
- Forward/backward and lateral integration
- Split steps, direction changes, acceleration
- 40 seconds high-intensity work
- Match-specific movement demands

## Performance Notes
- **Complex Integration**: Multiple exercises in sequence
- **Full-Body Focus**: Upper and lower body working together
- **System Challenge**: Cardiovascular and neuromuscular integration
- **Tennis Specificity**: Movement patterns match court demands
