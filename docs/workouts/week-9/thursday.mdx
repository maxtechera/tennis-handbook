## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Aerobic-Anaerobic Complex
- 3 minutes moderate pace (75% HRmax)
- Immediately into 30-second max sprint
- 90 seconds easy active recovery
- Challenges both energy systems
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Power Dead Bugs
- Explosive arm and leg extensions
- 45 seconds continuous work
- Focus on power and control
- Maintain core stability throughout
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Abs Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Plank Sprints
- Plank position with alternating knee drives
- 30 seconds maximum effort
- Simulate running motion in plank
- Keep hips stable throughout
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Core Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Mountain Climbers
- Rapid knee-to-chest alternating movement
- 45 seconds continuous effort
- Maintain plank position
- Focus on speed and control
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Court Sprints
- 20-second all-out sprints
- Tennis court length if available
- Focus on acceleration and speed
- Simulate match sprint demands
- Maximum effort each sprint

### Lateral Shuffles
- 30 seconds side-to-side movement
- Stay low in athletic position
- Quick feet with control
- Simulate defensive tennis movement
- Maintain balance throughout

## Performance Notes
- **Complex Training**: Multiple systems working together
- **Energy System Integration**: Aerobic and anaerobic combination
- **Tennis Specificity**: Movement patterns match tennis demands
- **Progressive Overload**: Increased complexity from previous weeks
