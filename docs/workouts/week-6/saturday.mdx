# Saturday - Week 6

_Power Mastery Phase - Competition or Power Recovery_

## Saturday Options

### Option A: Match Day (Recommended)
- **Power Competition Warm-up**: 20 minutes structured preparation
- **High-Intensity Match Play**: Apply week's power training
- **Comprehensive Recovery**: Extended post-match protocols

### Option B: Power Recovery Day
- **Zone-2 Power Walk**: 30 minutes at moderate pace
- **Power Movement Flow**: 20 minutes dynamic mobility
- **Recovery Breathing**: 10 minutes structured breathing

## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Power Competition Warm-up
- 5 minutes general activation
- 10 minutes power-specific movements
- 5 minutes progressive tennis hitting
- Mental preparation for power application
- Competition readiness protocols
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Warm-up Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Zone-2 Power Walk
- 30 minutes brisk walking pace
- Slightly higher intensity than easy jog
- Maintain conversational breathing
- Active recovery from power training
- Prepare for next week's strength phase
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Power Movement Flow
- Dynamic movements targeting power patterns
- Include hip circles, leg swings, arm circles
- Focus on range of motion used in power exercises
- 20 minutes comprehensive movement prep
- Maintain mobility from intense training
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Mobility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Recovery Breathing
- 10 minutes structured breathing exercise
- Focus on parasympathetic activation
- Deep diaphragmatic breathing patterns
- Mental recovery from training stress
- Prepare for upcoming strength phase

## Performance Notes
- **Match Focus**: Apply power training in competition
- **Recovery Option**: Moderate activity if no match
- **Phase Transition**: Prepare for maximum strength phase
- **Flexibility Priority**: Maintain mobility after power work
