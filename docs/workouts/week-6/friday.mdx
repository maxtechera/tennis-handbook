## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Court Power Sprints (8×15 sec)
- Maximum sprint intensity
- Tennis court length if available
- Focus on acceleration and top speed
- Full recovery between sprints
- Simulate match sprint demands

### Trap-bar Deadlift (5×3 @ 90%)
- Heavy load for maximum strength
- Explosive intention on every rep
- Perfect form required
- 3 reps only for power focus
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Deadlift Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=op9kVnSso6Q)

### Box Jumps (Maximum Height)
- Highest box you can safely clear
- Explosive triple extension
- Soft landing on box
- Step down between reps
- Maximum height each jump
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

### Hip Thrusts (5×5 @ 85%)
- Heavy load with explosive intent
- Maximum hip drive velocity
- Full glute activation
- Control eccentric phase
- Power development focus
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Hip Thrust Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xDmFkJxPzeM)

### Broad Jumps (Maximum Distance)
- Explosive horizontal jump
- Use arms for momentum
- Land with soft knees
- Measure distance for progression
- Maximum effort each rep
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

### Push Press (4×4 @ 85%)
- Heavy load with explosive drive
- Generate power from legs
- Fast bar velocity upward
- Stable catch position
- Maximum intended velocity
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Push Press Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iaBVSJm78ko)

## Performance Notes
- **Maximum Loads**: 85-90% 1RM for strength component
- **Power Expression**: Explosive movement immediately after strength
- **Full Recovery**: Complete rest between power sets
- **Quality Focus**: Perfect technique at high loads
