## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Push Press
- Setup with barbell at shoulder level
- Small knee dip then explosive drive
- Press overhead as legs extend
- Focus on transferring leg power to arms
- Control descent back to shoulders

### Medicine Ball Slam
- Hold ball overhead with extended arms
- Use entire body to slam ball down
- Maximum velocity and force
- Quick retrieval and reset
- Express the power from push press

### Explosive Push-ups
- Standard push-up position
- Lower with control
- Explode up so hands leave ground
- Land softly and reset
- Focus on maximum speed

### Bent-over Row
- Hip hinge, flat back position
- Pull bar to lower ribcage
- Squeeze shoulder blades together
- Control negative slowly
- Heavy load for strength

### Medicine Ball Pull-downs
- Overhead position with med ball
- Pull ball down explosively to chest
- Use lats and core maximally
- Reset quickly for next rep
- Express pulling power

## Performance Notes
- **Complex Training**: Heavy strength immediately followed by explosive power

- **Load**: 80% on strength exercises
