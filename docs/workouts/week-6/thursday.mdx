## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Bike Power Intervals (6×2 min)
- Higher intensity than previous weeks
- Target 85% maximum heart rate
- 90 seconds active recovery pedaling
- Focus on power output consistency
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Explosive Dead Bugs
- Lie on back, arms and knees at 90 degrees
- Explosively extend opposite arm and leg
- Quick return to starting position
- Focus on speed and control
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Abs Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Power Planks
- Standard plank position
- Add dynamic arm or leg movements
- Maintain core stability throughout
- 30 seconds of continuous movement
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Core Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Court Sprint Intervals
- 15-second all-out sprints
- Tennis-specific movement patterns
- Focus on acceleration and deceleration
- Simulate match movement demands
- Full recovery between sprints

### Lateral Power Steps
- Explosive lateral steps
- 10 steps each direction
- Focus on power and control
- Simulate wide tennis shots
- Quick direction changes

## Performance Notes
- **Power Focus**: Higher intensity aerobic work
- **Tennis Integration**: Movement patterns specific to tennis
- **Progressive Overload**: Increased from previous weeks
