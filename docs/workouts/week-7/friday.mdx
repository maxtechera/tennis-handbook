## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Trap-bar Deadlift (6×3 @ 92%)
- Very heavy load (92% 1RM)
- Maximum strength focus
- Perfect form required
- Explosive intention despite heavy load
- 3 reps for strength-power
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Deadlift Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=op9kVnSso6Q)

### Depth Jumps (Reactive)
- 18-24 inch box height
- Step off, land, immediately jump
- Minimal ground contact time
- Express strength as reactive power
- Focus on stretch-shortening cycle
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

### Hip Thrusts (5×4 @ 90%)
- Very heavy load for maximum strength
- Explosive hip drive intention
- Full glute activation
- Control eccentric slowly
- Bridge strength to power
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Hip Thrust Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xDmFkJxPzeM)

### Push Press (5×3 @ 90%)
- Maximum strength load
- Generate power from legs
- Heavy but explosive intent
- Perfect overhead position
- Strength with speed component
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Push Press Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iaBVSJm78ko)

### Explosive Push-ups
- Bodyweight power expression
- Hands leave ground on push
- Land softly and control
- Express upper body power
- Perfect form priority
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Push Up Variations](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ae-sOQhp0wQ)

### Heavy Farmer's Carry (60m)
- Heaviest load possible for distance
- Maintain perfect posture
- Functional strength application
- Core stability under load
- Progressive overload focus
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Farmer's Walk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p8FWv6bUJ7w)

## Performance Notes
- **Maximum Loads**: 90-92% 1RM for pure strength
- **Strength-Power Bridge**: Heavy lifting followed by explosive movement
- **Extended Rest**: 4-5 minutes for complete recovery
- **Perfect Form**: Critical at maximum loads
