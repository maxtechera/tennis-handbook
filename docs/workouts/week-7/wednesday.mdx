## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Bench Press (Heavy)
- Perfect setup on bench
- Tight shoulder blade retraction
- Lower bar with control to chest
- Drive feet into ground, press explosively
- Focus on maximum force production

### Bent-over Row (Heavy)
- Perfect hip hinge setup
- Pull bar to sternum with control
- Maximum force on each rep
- Feel the strength in upper back
- Control the negative phase

### Overhead Press
- Start with bar at shoulder level
- Tight core and glutes
- Press straight up, no lean back
- Lock out overhead position
- Control descent to shoulders

### Weighted Pull-ups
- Add weight via belt or vest
- Full hang to full pull-up
- Chest to bar if possible
- Control descent slowly
- Focus on lat and rhomboid strength

### Close-grip Push-ups
- Hands closer than shoulder width
- Lower to chest with control
- Press up focusing on triceps
- Maximum reps with perfect form
- Stop when form breaks down

## Performance Notes
- **Heavy Loads**: 85-90% 1RM requires perfect form
- **Rest Periods**: Full recovery between heavy sets
- **Spotting**: Use spotter for bench press
- **Progressive Overload**: Track weights for progression
