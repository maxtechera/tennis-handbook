# Sunday - Week 7

_Maximum Strength Phase - Critical Recovery_

## Maximum Strength Recovery Protocol

### Recovery & Nutrition Week 7 (Strength Phase)
- **Protein**: 2.2-2.5 g/kg for strength adaptations
- **Hydration**: 3+ liters daily, strength training demands
- **Sleep**: 9+ hours critical for strength recovery
- **Strength Recovery**: Priority on complete restoration

## Quick Recovery Instructions

### Restorative Yoga (40 minutes)
- Extended session for strength recovery
- Focus on hip flexors and shoulders
- Counter strength training tightness
- Deep relaxation and flexibility
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Mobility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Gentle Walk (45 minutes)
- Extended, very easy pace
- Focus on mental relaxation
- Recovery from heavy strength training
- No intensity whatsoever
- Mental break from training stress

### Deep Recovery Rolling (30 minutes)
- Extended foam rolling session
- Target areas stressed by heavy lifting
- Include full body attention
- Gentle, therapeutic pressure
- Critical for strength training recovery
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Recovery](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Extended Breathwork (20 minutes)
- Longer stress reduction session
- Multiple breathing techniques
- Recovery from training intensity
- Nervous system restoration
- Prepare for advanced methods phase

### Strength Assessment & Planning
- Review strength gains from the week
- Note areas of improvement
- Mental preparation for advanced methods
- Plan recovery strategies
- Assess strength training adaptations

## Key Focus Points
- **Critical Recovery**: Maximum strength training is demanding
- **Extended Sessions**: Longer recovery activities
- **Complete Rest**: No structured training stress
- **Preparation**: Ready for advanced methods phase
