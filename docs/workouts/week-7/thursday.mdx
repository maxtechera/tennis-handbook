## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Zone-1 Bike (30 min)
- Very comfortable, easy pace
- 60-65% maximum heart rate
- Should feel refreshing, not fatiguing
- Focus on smooth pedaling
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Supported Dead Bugs
- Lie on back with support under lower back
- Gentle arm and leg extensions
- Focus on control over intensity
- No explosive movements
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Abs Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Wall Sits
- Back against wall, knees at 90 degrees
- Hold position comfortably
- Focus on isometric strength maintenance
- Breathe normally throughout
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Leg Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4EkKhkSkGmQ)

### Dynamic Stretching
- Gentle range of motion movements
- Focus on major muscle groups
- Hold stretches 15-30 seconds
- Emphasize relaxation over intensity
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Mobility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Tennis Technical Precision
- Focus on perfect technique
- Low intensity, high quality
- Stroke mechanics refinement
- Court positioning practice
- Mental focus development

## Performance Notes
