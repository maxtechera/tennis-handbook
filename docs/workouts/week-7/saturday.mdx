# Saturday - Week 7

_Maximum Strength Phase - Active Recovery Priority_

## Saturday Options

### Option A: Light Match Play (If Available)
- **Extended Warm-up**: 25 minutes gentle preparation
- **Moderate Match Intensity**: Technical focus over power
- **Extended Recovery**: 20 minutes comprehensive cool-down

### Option B: Active Recovery (Recommended)
- **Gentle Zone-1 Activity**: 30 minutes very easy movement
- **Extended Mobility**: 25 minutes comprehensive stretching
- **Relaxation Protocol**: 15 minutes stress reduction

## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Extended Warm-up (Light Match)
- 25 minutes gentle preparation
- Focus on joint mobility and circulation
- No high-intensity movements
- Mental preparation for technical tennis
- Conservative approach due to strength training
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Warm-up Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Gentle Zone-1 Activity
- 30 minutes very easy movement
- Walking, light cycling, or swimming
- 50-60% maximum heart rate
- Should feel refreshing, not tiring
- Active recovery from heavy strength training
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Active Recovery](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Extended Mobility (25 minutes)
- Comprehensive stretching routine
- Hold each stretch 60-90 seconds
- Focus on areas tight from strength training
- Include hip flexors, hamstrings, shoulders
- Counteract strength training adaptations
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Stretching Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Relaxation Protocol (15 minutes)
- Progressive muscle relaxation
- Deep breathing exercises
- Mental stress reduction techniques
- Recovery from training intensity
- Prepare for continued strength training

### Extended Recovery (Match Day)
- 20 minutes comprehensive cool-down
- Extended static stretching
- Focus on recovery optimization
- Hydration and nutrition protocols
- Prepare for Sunday rest

## Performance Notes
- **Recovery Priority**: Active recovery over competition
- **Gentle Movement**: Avoid high-intensity activities
- **Flexibility Focus**: Counter strength training effects
- **Mental Recovery**: Reduce training stress
