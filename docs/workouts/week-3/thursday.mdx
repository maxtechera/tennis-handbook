## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Aerobic Bike Intervals (5×3 min)
- Increased volume from Week 2 (5 rounds vs 4)
- Maintain steady state intensity
- Focus on controlled, rhythmic breathing
- 90 seconds active recovery between intervals
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Hanging Leg Raises (3×12)
- Secure grip on pull-up bar
- Controlled knee-to-chest movement
- Hold 2 seconds at top position
- Slow eccentric lowering phase
- Minimal swing or momentum
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Abs Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Side Plank (3×45 sec)
- Extended hold time from previous weeks
- Perfect hip-to-shoulder alignment
- Stack shoulders and hips vertically
- Breathe normally throughout
- Focus on lateral core stability
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Core Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Sun Salutations
- Flow through dynamic yoga sequence
- Target hip flexors and shoulders
- Emphasize smooth transitions
- Hold poses briefly at end ranges
- Focus on mobility over speed

### Tennis Technical Drills
- Practice slice with underspin control
- Dropshot precision and placement
- Controlled groundstroke consistency
- Emphasize technique over power
- Work on court positioning

### Zone-2 Aerobic Bike (30 min)
- Maintain conversational pace
- 60-70% maximum heart rate
- Comfortable, sustainable effort
- Focus on efficiency and form
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Tibialis Raises
- Stand with heels on step, toes hanging
- Lift toes toward shins maximally
- Control descent slowly
- Feel work in shin muscles
- Important for ankle stability
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Calf Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mTlnrjbfhso)

## Key Focus Points

- **Aerobic Intervals**: Increased from Week 2 (5×3 min vs 4×3 min) at steady state
- **Core Stability**: Extended plank holds (45 seconds) with controlled leg raises
- **Mobility Flow**: Sun salutations with targeted hip and shoulder work
- **Technical Precision**: Slice, dropshot, and controlled groundstroke development
- **Aerobic Base**: Zone-2 work maintaining conversational pace
- **Tendon Care**: Deep stretching and tibialis strengthening

## Notes

Thursday continues as a recovery day with progressive aerobic capacity building and refined tennis technical work.
