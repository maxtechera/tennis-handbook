## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Push Press (4×6)
- Start with barbell at shoulder level
- Drive explosively with hips and legs
- Press bar overhead as legs extend
- Land stable with arms locked
- Focus on hip-to-shoulder power transfer

### Bent-over Row (4×6)
- Hinge at hips, flat back
- Pull bar to lower ribcage
- Squeeze shoulder blades together
- Control the negative descent
- Feel lats working, not just arms

### Neutral Grip DB Press (3×8)
- Hold dumbbells with palms facing each other
- Press straight up from shoulders
- Full range of motion
- Control both up and down phases
- Maintain core stability throughout

### Chin-ups (3×AMRAP)
- Underhand grip, hands shoulder-width apart
- Pull chest toward bar
- Control descent to full extension
- Stop when form breaks down
- Track total reps for progression

### Face Pulls (3×12)
- Cable at face height
- Pull to face with elbows high
- External rotation at end position
- Squeeze rear delts and rhomboids
- Smooth controlled movement

## Key Focus Points

- **Competitive Tennis**: High intensity sets with tactical strategy focus
- **Explosive Power**: Push press with explosive hip drive and stable landing
- **Lat Engagement**: Bent-over rows with controlled movement and lat focus
- **Neutral Grip**: DB press with full range and controlled tempo
- **Maximum Reps**: Chin-ups to failure with controlled full extension
- **Posterior Deltoids**: Face pulls with high elbows and smooth execution
- **High-Intensity Conditioning**: Sprint and kettlebell circuit
