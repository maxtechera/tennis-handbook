## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Trap-bar Deadlift (4×6)
- Strong pulling movement with neutral spine
- Hip hinge with explosive drive up
- Maintain chest up throughout movement
- Control weight down slowly
- Progressive load from Week 2
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Deadlift Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=op9kVnSso6Q)

### Hip Thrusts (4×8)
- Increased volume from previous weeks
- Explosive upward hip movement
- Squeeze glutes hard at top
- Control eccentric phase
- Full range of motion
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Hip Thrust Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xDmFkJxPzeM)

### Landmine Press (3×8 per side)
- One end of barbell in landmine base
- Press at 45-degree angle
- Stable core throughout movement
- Controlled motion both directions
- Unilateral strength development
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Landmine Press](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kR8gZcCK5-8)

### Rotational Med-Ball Slams (3×10)
- Full-body rotation emphasis
- Explosive slam with core engagement
- Lateral or diagonal slam patterns
- Maximum force on each rep
- Quick reset between reps
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Power Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K8aGBKy3a1k)

### Farmer's Walk (2×40m)
- Maintain upright posture throughout
- Firm grip without over-tension
- Controlled walking pace
- Core engaged to prevent lean
- Challenge grip strength progressively
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Farmer's Walk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p8FWv6bUJ7w)

### Reactive Plyometrics (3×6)
- Increased volume from Week 2
- Depth jumps with minimal ground contact
- Lateral bounds for lateral power
- Focus on landing mechanics
- Explosive takeoff each rep
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

## Key Focus Points

- **Dynamic Tennis**: Rapid transitions with explosive agility patterns
- **Deadlift Power**: Strong pulling movement with neutral spine maintenance
- **Hip Drive**: Explosive hip thrusts with increased volume (4×8)
- **Unilateral Press**: Landmine press with stable core and controlled motion
- **Rotational Power**: Med-ball slams with full-body rotation emphasis
- **Grip Endurance**: Farmer's walks maintaining upright posture
- **Reactive Training**: Increased plyometric volume (3×6 vs 3×5)

## Notes

Friday culminates the week with dynamic strength training and progressive plyometric development, emphasizing power and agility.
