### General Recovery Guidelines

- **Protein intake**: 1.8–2.2 g/kg daily.
- **Hydration**: Minimum 2 L water, plus electrolytes.
- **Sleep**: 7.5–9 hours/night.
- **Regular monitoring**: Adjust training intensity based on fatigue levels, soreness, and HRV readings.

Track your progress daily, note energy levels, performance quality, and recovery status to inform future adjustments.
