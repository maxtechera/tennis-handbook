**Romanian Deadlift (2×8)**: Perfect hip hinge pattern, moderate weight

- **Reduced Intensity**: Moderate tennis drills focusing on rhythm and consistency

- **Form Focus**: Emphasis on controlled movement and proper technique
- **Recovery**: Easy intensity with controlled breathing
- **Movement Quality**: Prioritize quality over quantity

## Notes

Week 4 is a critical deload week designed for full recovery and adaptation, setting the stage for intensified training in upcoming phases.
