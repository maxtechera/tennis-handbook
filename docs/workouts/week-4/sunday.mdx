# Sunday – Rest & Comprehensive Recovery

## Sunday Recovery Protocol (Deload Week)

### Complete Rest Activities
- **Gentle yoga**: 30 minutes restorative practice
- **Leisure walk**: 30 minutes easy pace
- **Extended foam rolling**: Target all muscle groups
- **Breathwork exercises**: 15 minutes stress reduction

## Recovery & Nutrition Week 4 (Deload)
- **Protein**: Maintain at 1.8–2.2 g/kg body weight
- **Hydration**: Continue 2–3 liters water daily, electrolytes
- **Sleep**: Prioritize 8–9 hours per night
- **Monitoring**: Close attention to recovery markers

## Key Focus Points (Deload Week)

- **Complete Recovery**: Focus on full nervous system restoration
- **Sleep Optimization**: Extended sleep for adaptation
- **Stress Reduction**: Mental and physical relaxation techniques
- **Preparation**: Ready for Week 5 Development phase

## Notes

Deload week is critical for full recovery and adaptation, setting the stage for intensified training in upcoming Development phase.
