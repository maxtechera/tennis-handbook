# Saturday – Optional Light Activity

## Saturday Options (Deload Week)

### Recommended Activities
- **No matches recommended** during deload week
- **Gentle walking**: 20–30 minutes at easy pace
- **Stretching session**: 20–30 minutes comprehensive flexibility work
- **Yoga**: Gentle restorative practice

## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Gentle Walking (20-30 minutes)
- Easy pace, no exertion
- Fresh air and light movement
- Focus on relaxation and enjoyment
- Maintain circulation without stress
- Mental break from training
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Active Recovery](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Stretching Session (20-30 minutes)
- Hold each stretch 60-90 seconds
- Focus on tight areas from the week
- Include full-body flexibility work
- Deep breathing and relaxation
- Extended holds for flexibility gains
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Stretching Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Gentle Yoga
- Restorative yoga poses
- Focus on relaxation and flexibility
- Hold poses longer than usual
- Breathing-focused practice
- Mental and physical recovery
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Mobility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

## Key Focus Points (Deload Week)

- **Complete Rest from Competition**: No matches during deload
- **Gentle Movement**: Light activities for circulation
- **Flexibility Focus**: Extended stretching and mobility work
- **Mental Recovery**: Relaxation and stress reduction

## Notes

Saturday emphasizes complete rest from competitive activities while maintaining gentle movement for recovery.
