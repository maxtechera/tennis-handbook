## Workout Structure

### Phase 1: Integrated Power Assessment (20 min)
**Power Application Readiness**

**Focus**: Assess power application capacity

**Key Elements**:
- HRV measurement with power application analysis
- Integrated power movement screen
- CNS power application capacity assessment
- Power application focus evaluation

**Progression**: Address application-specific needs, activate integrated power systems

### Phase 2: Tennis Precision Training (75 min)
**Power with Precision Integration**

**Focus**: Combine explosive power with technical precision

**Warm-up (15 min)**:
- Power precision court movement patterns
- Integrated stroke mechanics preparation
- Power precision ball contact prep

**Main Training (50 min)**:
- **Power Precision Drills**: Explosive shots with accuracy targets
- **Precision Serves**: Maximum power with placement demands
- **Precision Volleys**: Power volleys with target accuracy
- **Precision Under Pressure**: Maintain power and precision in competition

**Success Metrics**:
- Power precision: Maximum power with 80% accuracy
- Integrated performance: Power and precision combined
- Competition precision: 85%+ precision under pressure

### Phase 3: Power Application Preparation (20 min)
**Integrated Power Prep**

**Focus**: Prepare for integrated power application

**Key Elements**:
- Power application activation patterns
- Dynamic integrated mobility
- Core power application activation
- Integrated power pattern rehearsal
