## Key Coaching Points

### Technical Focus
- **Total Body Coordination**: Integrate all movement patterns explosively
- **Explosive Intent**: Maximum velocity in all exercises
- **Movement Quality**: Perfect technique at explosive velocities
- **Tennis Integration**: Transfer explosive power to tennis movements

### Safety Considerations
- **Form Priority**: Never compromise technique for explosiveness
- **Progressive Loading**: Build explosive capacity gradually
- **Recovery Focus**: Adequate rest between explosive efforts
- **Injury Prevention**: Maintain movement quality standards

### Progression Markers
- All explosive sets completed with maximum intent
- Tennis explosiveness maintained throughout session
- Total body explosive quality sustained
- Power integration demonstrated across all movements

## Level Modifications

### Beginner
- **Volume**: Reduce to 3 sets main exercises
- **Intensity**: 50-60% 1RM with explosive focus
- **Tennis**: Focus on technique over explosiveness
- **Recovery**: Extend recovery periods

### Intermediate
- **Volume**: As prescribed
- **Intensity**: As prescribed
- **Tennis**: As prescribed
- **Recovery**: As prescribed

### Advanced
- **Volume**: Add 1 set to main exercises
- **Intensity**: 70-80% 1RM with explosive tracking
- **Tennis**: Increase explosive demands
- **Recovery**: Add explosive techniques
