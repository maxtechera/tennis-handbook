# Sunday – Rest & Recovery

## Sunday Recovery Protocol

### Active Recovery Options
- **Gentle Yoga**: 30 minutes focusing on flexibility and relaxation
- **Leisurely Walk**: 30 minutes at easy pace, preferably outdoors

### Recovery Techniques
- **Extensive foam rolling**: Target all muscle groups worked during the week
- **Breathwork exercises**: 10-15 minutes of focused breathing techniques
- **Self-massage**: Address any tight or sore areas

## Weekly Nutrition & Recovery Summary

### Recovery & Nutrition Week 2
- **Daily protein**: 1.8–2.2 g/kg body weight
- **Hydration**: 2–3 liters water daily, electrolytes as needed
- **Sleep**: 7.5–9 hours per night
- **Monitoring**: Track fatigue, soreness, and HRV for training adjustments

## Quick Recovery Instructions

### Gentle Yoga (30 minutes)
- Focus on flexibility and relaxation
- Hold poses 30-60 seconds
- Emphasize deep breathing throughout
- Include hip openers and spinal twists
- End with 5 minutes of relaxation
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Mobility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Leisurely Walk (30 minutes)
- Easy pace outdoors if possible
- No intensity or time pressure
- Focus on fresh air and mental relaxation
- Promote gentle blood circulation
- Mindful walking and enjoyment

### Extensive Foam Rolling
- Target all muscle groups from the week
- Spend extra time on tight areas
- Gentle pressure, avoid pain
- Include calves, quads, glutes, lats
- 15-20 minutes total session
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Recovery](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

### Breathwork Exercises (10-15 minutes)
- Deep diaphragmatic breathing
- 4-7-8 breathing pattern (inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8)
- Focus on parasympathetic activation
- Reduce stress and promote recovery
- Can be done lying down or seated

### Self-Massage
- Address tight or sore areas
- Use hands, tennis ball, or massage tools
- Gentle pressure and circular motions
- Focus on shoulders, neck, feet
- 10-15 minutes as needed

## Key Focus Points

- **Complete Rest**: No structured training, focus on recovery
- **Gentle Movement**: Light activity to promote blood flow
- **Stress Reduction**: Relaxation techniques and breathing exercises
- **Nutrition Focus**: Adequate protein and hydration for recovery
- **Sleep Optimization**: Prioritize quality sleep for adaptation

## Notes

Document daily progress, noting performance metrics, perceived exertion, and recovery status for informed adaptations in Week 3.
