## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Bike Intervals (4×3 min)
- Increase intensity 10% from Week 1
- 90 seconds recovery between intervals
- Maintain steady cadence and form
- Focus on controlled breathing throughout
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Hanging Leg Raises (3×12)
- Hang from pull-up bar with secure grip
- Raise knees toward chest in controlled motion
- Hold for 2 seconds at top position
- Control descent slowly to starting position
- Avoid swinging or using momentum
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Abs Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Side Plank (3×30 sec)
- Perfect alignment from hip to shoulder
- Stack hips and shoulders vertically
- Breathe normally throughout hold
- Progress from Week 1 duration
- Focus on core stability
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Core Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_xdOuqokcm4)

### Tennis Slice Drills
- Use low-to-high swing path
- Generate underspin with racquet angle
- Focus on placement accuracy over power
- Practice cross-court and down-the-line
- Emphasize consistent ball contact

### Zone-2 Cardio (30 min)
- Maintain 60-70% maximum heart rate
- Should be able to hold conversation
- Comfortable, sustainable pace
- Focus on nasal breathing if possible
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Cardio Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kl0ht3ow8TQ)

### Couch Stretch
- Place rear foot elevated on couch/bench
- Push hips forward to feel hip flexor stretch
- Hold for 90 seconds each side
- Keep torso upright throughout
- Important for hip mobility
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Mobility](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8wPpK6xW9s8)

## Key Focus Points

- **Bike Intervals**: Slightly increased intensity from Week 1, maintain steady breathing
- **Core Strength**: Controlled hanging leg raises with minimal swing
- **Side Stability**: Maintain proper alignment during side planks
- **Mobility Work**: Gentle yoga flows targeting hips and shoulders
- **Tennis Precision**: Slice and precision drills with controlled execution
- **Aerobic Base**: Zone-2 cardio maintaining conversational pace
- **Tendon Care**: Extended mobility focusing on couch stretch and tibialis raises

## Notes

Thursday serves as a recovery day with moderate conditioning work and technical tennis development, emphasizing precision over power.
