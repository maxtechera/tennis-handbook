## Quick Exercise Instructions

**Barbell Bench Press (4×6)**: Setup flat bench, grip wider than shoulders, lower to chest slowly, press explosively, Week 2 load increase

**T-Bar Row (4×8)**: Flat back position, pull handle to lower ribs, squeeze shoulder blades, control negative

**DB Shoulder Press (3×8)**: Seated/standing, press dumbbells overhead, core tight, full ROM

**Chin-ups (3×AMRAP)**: Underhand grip, pull chest to bar, control descent, max reps each set

**Face Pulls (3×12)**: Cable at face height, pull to face with elbows high, squeeze rear delts

**HIIT Finisher**: 30s sprint intervals + 30s KB swings, 90s rest between rounds, 3 total rounds

## Key Focus Points

- **Competitive Tennis**: Practice sets with competitive intensity and tactical focus
- **Heavy Upper Body**: Barbell bench press with heavier loads while maintaining control
- **Posterior Chain**: T-Bar rows with flat back and controlled pulls
- **Shoulder Stability**: Strict form on DB shoulder press with core engagement
- **Pull-up Strength**: Full ROM chin-ups to muscular failure (AMRAP)
- **High-Intensity Finish**: HIIT finisher with sprint intervals and kettlebell swings

## Notes

Wednesday is the most demanding upper body session with competitive tennis followed by heavy lifting and high-intensity metabolic work.
