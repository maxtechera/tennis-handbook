## Quick Exercise Instructions

### Trap-bar Deadlift (4×6)
- Week 2 load increase from previous week
- Hip hinge pattern with neutral spine
- Explosive drive through heels and hips
- Control descent slowly and deliberately
- Focus on perfect form with heavier weight
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Deadlift Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=op9kVnSso6Q)

### Hip Thrusts (3×10)
- Shoulders on bench, feet flat on floor
- Explosive hip drive, squeeze glutes hard
- Control descent slowly
- Create straight line from knees to shoulders
- Focus on posterior chain power
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Hip Thrust Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xDmFkJxPzeM)

### Push Press (3×8)
- Start with slight knee dip
- Explosive leg drive to initiate movement
- Press bar overhead in one fluid motion
- Land stable with arms locked out
- Generate power from lower body
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Push Press Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iaBVSJm78ko)

### Med-Ball Slams (3×10)
- Hold ball overhead with both hands
- Slam down hard with maximum force
- Full body engagement from core
- Explosive intent on every rep
- Quick retrieval for next rep
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Power Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K8aGBKy3a1k)

### Farmer's Carry (2×40m)
- Heavy loads challenging your grip
- Maintain upright posture throughout
- Stable grip without over-squeezing
- Take controlled steps forward
- Core engaged to prevent leaning
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Farmer's Walk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p8FWv6bUJ7w)

### Depth Jumps (3×5)
- Step off box (12-18 inches high)
- Land and immediately jump up maximally
- Minimal ground contact time
- Focus on reactive strength
- Soft landing then explosive takeoff
- **Video Reference**: [Jeff Nippard - Plyometric Training](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tcY1dI5-Teo)

## Key Focus Points

- **Tennis Agility**: Aggressive transitions with quick lateral movements
- **Deadlift Power**: Increased load from Week 1 with controlled execution
- **Hip Drive**: Explosive hip thrusts with controlled eccentric phase
- **Overhead Power**: Push press generating power from hips with full extension
- **Explosive Throws**: Med-ball slams with full-body engagement
- **Grip Strength**: Farmer's carry maintaining upright posture
- **Plyometric Power**: Reactive plyometrics with minimal ground contact time

## Notes

Friday caps off the week with explosive power development, combining tennis agility work with heavy lifting and reactive plyometrics.
