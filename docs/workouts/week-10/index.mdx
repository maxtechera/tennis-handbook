# Week 10 - Peak Power & Competition Intensity

## Week 10 Objectives (Intensification Phase)

Build toward peak power expression while maintaining high tennis intensity. This week focuses on:

- **Peak Power Development**: Maximum power output with competition loads
- **Tennis Competition**: High-intensity match play and tactical execution
- **Neural Drive**: Maximum explosive capacity development
- **Competition Preparation**: Tournament-level tennis intensity
- **Power Transfer**: Optimal gym-to-court power transfer

## Training Schedule

- **Monday**: Peak Lower Power & Competition Tennis - Maximum squat power + competitive rallies
- **Tuesday**: Upper Power & Serve Dominance - Peak bench power + serve speed focus
- **Wednesday**: Competition Simulation & Power Endurance - Tournament simulation + power complexes
- **Thursday**: Recovery & Technical Mastery - Light recovery + technical precision
- **Friday**: Maximum Explosive & Court Speed - Peak deadlift power + court agility
- **Saturday**: Tournament Competition - Full competition intensity
- **Sunday**: Active Recovery & Assessment - Light movement + performance review
